This is a little memo about different styles and typedface that can be applied while creating websites.

What is typeface?

There are many kinds of fonts that can be used but not all of them are rendered well by the browsers. That's why it's better to use safe fonts, such as Google Fonts. They can be divided into two kinds: serif and sans-serif.

Use of colors

There are different ways to present colors, such as RGB, HSL, HSV, hex and many others. They are much better than using just names of the colors, as the names of colors are limited, less than 150. But the variety of colors shown through hex and HSL is much wider.
